Our Organizers

Enterprise Ireland

Hosting Organization

Enterprise Ireland is a government organization dedicated to supporting Irish businesses in expanding both locally and internationally. Through initiatives like this annual book fair, Enterprise Ireland not only fosters a vibrant reading culture but also strengthens global business connections, offering a platform for Irish authors, publishers, and entrepreneurs to showcase their work. This event enables networking with international stakeholders, promoting Irish literature and culture on a global scale while driving opportunities for business collaboration and cultural exchange.

  • Department: Cultural Events & Business Development
  • Email: bookfair@enterprise-ireland.com
  • Phone: +353 2 123 4567
Higher Education Ireland

Educational Partner

Higher Education Ireland operates under the guidance of the Higher Education Authority (HEA), Ireland's primary body responsible for funding, regulating, and supporting the development of higher education institutions. It plays a pivotal role in promoting access to higher education, developing policies for higher education and research, a nd ensuring the sector's alignment with national and EU objectives. The HEA also works on numerous initiatives to advance skills, workforce engagement, and equity within Irish higher education. For more information, you can contact the HEA directly through their main office:

  • Department: Cultural & Academic Events
  • Email: events@hei.ie
  • Phone: +353 2 123 4568
