Cambridge University Press

Cambridge University Press is a renowned academic publisher, bringing the latest in scholarly books, journals, and online resources. They are dedicated to advancing knowledge and learning in various academic fields. Visit their booth to explore books across disciplines like science, literature, and history.

Featured Products: Academic textbooks, educational software, online courses

Hong Kong Book Fair

The Hong Kong Book Fair is an annual event showcasing thousands of books and publishing resources. It attracts publishers, authors, and readers from around the world. The exhibition features the latest in both academic and entertainment literature.

Featured Products: International book releases, literary events, author signings

Penguin House

Penguin House is one of the most iconic publishing houses in the world, known for its range of bestsellers, novels, and non-fiction books. They publish books from various genres, including contemporary fiction, classic novels, and cookbooks. Stop by their booth to discover their latest releases.

Featured Products: Fiction novels, memoirs, cookbooks, children’s books
